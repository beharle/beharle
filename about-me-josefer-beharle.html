Biography Joséfer Beharle
  

Joséfer Beharle specialize in digitalizing national libraries and museums, with the aim of spreading culture digitally throughout the world. He is a veteran of developing and managing big data content to build new informational technologies that improve the accessibility for organizations and individuals. He graduated from the National University of Mexico with a Degree in Librarianship and Information Studies at the Faculty of Philosophy & Literature and a Master Degree in Science in Information Technology. 

He began an early and long career with the Mexican government, where he worked as the National Director of IT for the Mexican Presidency, Interior Minister, Transport and Communications Minister, Labour Minister and Culture Minister. He subsequently moved into the corporate sphere, where he managed a slew of blue-chip clients including the Coca-Cola Company, the American Express Company, Walmart Corporate and Second Wind Consultants Incorporate, among many other. 

As visual designer Josefer Beharle is a professional image, photo, video and sound producer as well as visual artist who has participate in different social and cultural events like for example on BYOB, at the Ex Teresa Modern Art Museum in Mexico City, (March 4, 2011).
